Social Media Optimization

As a client using SMO you can see vast increases in your page rank and click through rate with these 5 simple tips.

First of all let's explain Social Media Optimization simply. The idea is twofold increase your page rank in google or other search engines by raising your “trust score” ie the number of links from external pages that your site has increases a trust score within the google internal servers. One way to get other sites to link to your content is to get clicks using Social media. Getting a post to the Front Page of Reddit is worth about $7500. Clickbait articles can increase your click through rate somewhere between 50% and 500%. According to Peter Koechley, the Co-Founder of Upworthy.com, “The difference between a good headline and a bad headline can be just massive. It’s not a rounding error. When we test headlines we see 20% difference, 50% difference, 500% difference. A really excellent headline can make something go viral.” Providing good content - this one is obvious but also super essential. If you have good content it's easy to translate to click through rates and For Social Media Optimization to work you need to have content that will get reshared thought links in social media. Which again all depends on good content. Make your content easily shareable. Having but the buttons to share something to twitter for example have gotten so ubiquitous it's almost annoying at this point. However there is a reason that they have gotten so ubiquitous as well.
